
Welcome
==========================================
These tutorials provide an introduction to the QGIS software. They are pitched at the level of an of introductory college level GIS course.

You don't need to know anything about GIS to begin this course. This is a completely beginner-friendly course.

Topics covered by the different tutorials thematic mapping, loading data from different sources, map projections, attribute and spatial queries, terrain 
mapping and analysis, geocoding, digitizing, geoprocessing, spatial interpolation, and spatial statistics.
